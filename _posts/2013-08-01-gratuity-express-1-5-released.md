Gratuity Express 1.5 has been released.

**What's new:**

- Support for archiving tips. This is implemented using Core Data for storage. Everything stays on the device for now with the potential of supporting iCloud Sync in the future.
- Ability to remove ads from the free version using in-app payments.
- Cosmetic and usability improvements.

<img class="center-block" alt="Gratuity Express 1.5 Main Screen" src="{{ site.url }}/blog_assets/iPhone5-Main_s1.png" width="194" height="416" />

Check out the [Gratuity Express site](http://oscaraperez.com/gratuityexpress/) to see all the features.  Get Gratuity Express 1.5 Free in the [App Store](https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/gratuity-express-tip-calculator/id593425970)!

The next release will include iOS 7 support which brings extensive design and layout changes. Send any feedback on Twitter [@oscrperez](https://twitter.com/oscrperez).
