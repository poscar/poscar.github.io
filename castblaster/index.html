CastBlaster allows you to send any media on your computer to your Chromecast device.

Easy, simple, and quick

Send your local videos and music to your Chromecast on Linux, Mac and Windows.

  • Linux
  • Mac
  • Windows

Features:

  • Cast any video or audio file on your computer. We support all the common containers and codecs.
  • Seek media.
  • Drag and Drop.

Requirements:

  • Linux, Mac, or Windows
  • Google Chrome with Google Cast extension.

FAQ:

My Chromecast device doesn't show up in CastBlaster, what gives?

There are many reasons why your device doesn't show up, including:

  • The browser or extension are having issues detecting the chromecast device or connecting to the receiver app. Try typing chrome://restart in your address bar and try again. This will restart chrome.
  • Your firewall is preventing your computer from reaching your chromecast.
  • You're not trusting the network you're connected to. Make sure you trust the network you're connected to. This means selecting 'Home' instead of 'Public' on Windows.
